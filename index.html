Privacy Policy

Updated on November 10, 2025

Introduction

Simple Aussie Weather, an application developed by Logistics Oasis Limited ("we", "our", or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use Simple Aussie Weather (the "App").

Information We Collect

Simple Aussie Weather handles personal and sensitive user data (location) in accordance with the Google Play User Data Policy.

Location Data

We request access to your device's location to automatically detect your nearest Australian city and provide accurate local weather forecasts.

How We Use Location Data:

  • Location data we collect through this app is only used to determine your nearest city from our pre-defined list of Australian locations.
  • The location is processed locally on your device.
  • We do not track your location movements or history.
  • Third-party advertising partners (AdMob) may use your IP address to find an approximate location as described in their policies. Though precise location information is not provided.

Your Control:

  • Granting this app location permission is optional.
  • If you deny location permission, the app defaults to showing the weather forecasts for the default Australian location of Sydney.
  • You can manually select any Australian city from the dropdown menu at any time.
  • You can revoke location permissions through your device's settings.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party SDKs (Google AdMob, React Native libraries) that may process limited, non-personal data necessary for good functionality and analytics.

Google AdMob

Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob to request non-personalised ads only. Google AdMob may collect device information for the purpose of ad-related analytics and serving ads.

Information that AdMob may collect includes:

  • Device ID.
  • Device information (the model and/or operating system).
  • Ad interaction data.
  • Basic location information (at the country/regional level).
  • IP address.

For more information about Google's advertising practices, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Help

React Native and Associated Libraries

Our App uses these React Native and related libraries for the weather app's functionality:

  • geolocation (for location detection)
  • google-mobile-ads (for advertisements)
  • svg (for graphics)

These libraries do not transmit personal information to external servers beyond what is documented in this privacy policy.

Data Storage and Security

  • The Application does not maintain any user accounts or profiles.
  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers.
  • Weather data is temporarily cached on your device for display purposes only.

Minors

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

Your Rights and Choices

You, as the user of this app, have the right to:

  • Deny location permissions (the Application will still work with manual city selection).
  • Revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all of the locally cached data.
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions.

Opting Out of Personalised Ads

While our App requests non-personalised ads by default, you can more directly control ad personalisation through your device's settings:

For Android users: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Data Retention

  • Location data is not retained (it is used only momentarily to determine your nearest city).
  • Weather data is cached on your device temporarily and automatically refreshed when you use the application.
  • No historical data is stored.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

International Data Transfer Disclosures

Weather data requests (i.e., for city coordinates) may be transmitted internationally. Google AdMob services may also involve international data transfers as is described in Google's privacy policies.

Australian Privacy Principles Compliance

We are committed to complying with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988. This includes:

  • Collecting only necessary information.
  • Being transparent about our data practices.
  • Protecting the information we handle.
  • Providing you with access to and control over your information.

Location Consent Process

We process your location data based on your consent, which you provide when granting location permissions. You can withdraw this consent at any time by revoking the relevant location permissions on your Android device's settings page.
